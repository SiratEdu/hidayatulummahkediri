Tentang Yayasan

Yayasan Hidayatul Ummah Kediri adalah lembaga nirlaba resmi terdaftar di Indonesia di bawah kepemimpinan KH. Moh Siri. Kami menyediakan layanan pendidikan Islam dan kesejahteraan sosial untuk masyarakat dengan integritas profesional dan transparansi.

Visi kami adalah menciptakan generasi Muslim yang beriman, berilmu, dan berakhlak mulia, serta berkontribusi positif bagi masyarakat sekitar.

Yayasan kami aktif dalam berbagai kegiatan keagamaan, pendidikan anak, dan program sosial untuk membantu masyarakat kurang mampu.

Hidayatul Ummah Kediri Foundation is an officially registered non-profit organization in Indonesia led by KH. Moh Siri. We provide Islamic education and social welfare services to the community with professional integrity and transparency.

Our vision is to create a generation of Muslims who are faithful, knowledgeable, have good morals, and contribute positively to the surrounding community.

Our foundation is active in various religious activities, children's education, and social programs to help underprivileged communities.

KH. Moh Siri
